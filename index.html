Steak Options and Grilling Approach

Quality Cuts • Doneness Choice

Steaks are the foundation of the Outback Steakhouse Food Menu. Each steak is cut from quality beef and seasoned with a special blend that enhances the natural flavor. Guests choose their preferred doneness for a personalized experience. Different cuts vary in texture, richness, and size, and steaks are served with sides that complete the plate—one of the main reasons steak lovers trust Outback Steakhouse.

Pick your cut

Choose based on tenderness vs. marbling—different cuts offer different textures and richness.

Pick your doneness

Medium-rare to medium is often the sweet spot for tenderness and juiciness (but order what you love).

Chicken and Rib Dishes for Hearty Appetites

Comfort • Bold Sauces

Beyond steaks, the menu includes a strong selection of chicken and rib dishes. Chicken meals are typically grilled or seasoned to deliver strong flavor without being overly heavy. Ribs are slow-cooked for tenderness and coated with sauces that add depth and richness—perfect for diners who love comfort food with a bold taste profile.

Seafood Choices for a Lighter Option

Balanced • Flavorful

Seafood helps balance the Outback Steakhouse Food Menu. Options like grilled fish and shrimp-based dishes are ideal for guests who want a lighter meal without sacrificing flavor. Seasonings are used carefully to keep the natural seafood taste front and center, often paired with fresh sides for a clean and satisfying meal.

Side Dishes That Complete the Plate

Customization • Balance

Side dishes are essential for customization and balance. Guests can choose comfort sides or lighter vegetable options depending on preference. Each side is prepared to complement the main dish. This ability to mix and match adds flexibility and value to the dining experience.
